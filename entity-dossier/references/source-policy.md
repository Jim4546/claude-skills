# Source Policy

## Reliability Ladder

Use higher-tier sources first. Lower-tier sources can guide search but should not carry important claims alone.

| Tier | Use | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| A | Core facts, dates, legal status, disclosures | Company registry, official company site, exchange filings, prospectus, annual/interim reports, court/government databases, patent/trademark offices, university profile pages, official project notices |
| B | Corroboration and event detail | Reputable media, official WeChat/public account posts, government/park announcements, investor portfolio pages, conference agendas, academic databases |
| C | Clues only unless corroborated | Commercial data aggregators, scraped biographies, generic business directories, social profiles, reposted press releases, forum posts |

## China Search Checklist

- Company registry: 国家企业信用信息公示系统 plus public mirrors such as 爱企查、企查查、天眼查 when directly accessible.
- Securities and filings: 巨潮资讯, SSE, SZSE, BSE, HKEX, NEEQ, CSRC, local equity exchange disclosures.
- Courts and enforcement: 中国裁判文书网, 中国执行信息公开网, 信用中国, local government administrative penalty pages.
- IP and technical assets: 国家知识产权局, 中国及多国专利审查信息查询, Google Patents, trademark databases, standards/project notices.
- Financing and policy: official investor pages, government industry funds, local development zone notices, press releases, reputable deal databases with public snippets.
- People: university faculty/lab pages, prospectus/director biographies, listed-company announcements, patent inventor affiliation, papers, conference pages, official appointment announcements.

## Overseas Search Checklist

- Registries: SEC EDGAR, Companies House, OpenCorporates, state Secretary of State registries, SEDAR+, ASIC, ACRA, local corporate registries.
- Securities and markets: SEC/EDGAR, NASDAQ/NYSE issuer pages, HKEX, SGX, LSE, Euronext, OTC Markets where relevant.
- IP and technical assets: USPTO, EPO, WIPO, Google Patents, Lens, trademark registries.
- People and scholarship: university profile pages, ORCID, Google Scholar, OpenAlex, Crossref, conference programs, public board biographies.
- Financing: company/investor announcements, regulatory filings, reputable business media, public Crunchbase/PitchBook snippets only as clues unless corroborated.

## Evidence Handling

- Record source title, publisher, date or access date, URL, and the exact fact supported.
- Prefer primary sources when sources conflict. If the conflict matters, write the higher-confidence fact and note the conflict only in a compact `公开资料差异` row.
- Do not quote long passages. Paraphrase facts and keep short excerpts only when necessary.
- If a source is paywalled but a public snippet is visible, use it only for low-stakes clues or find a second source.
- For user-requested exclusions, do not include excluded entities in diagrams or narrative unless required to disambiguate the target.
