# Report Structure

Default title: `{公司简称}与{人物名}信息整理YYYYMMDD`.

## 1. 核心结论

- One dense paragraph or 3-5 bullets.
- Cover: target identity, company positioning, person relationship, equity/control signal, and any material public-data gaps.
- Use neutral information整理稿 language. Do not write investment advice unless asked.

## 2. 公司信息

### 公司基本信息

Use a table with: company full name, aliases, founded/registered date, registered capital, legal representative, actual controller if confirmed, registered address, business scope, website/public channels, current status, and source.

### 公司沿革与关键公开事件

Use a chronological table:

| Date | Event | Evidence |
|---|---|---|

Include registration/name changes, product launches, financing, policy awards, major partnerships, IP milestones, regulatory/court events, and public announcements.

### 融资、政策与资本线索

Summarize confirmed rounds, investors, government funds/projects, grants, industrial-park support, and listed-company relationships. If financing is unconfirmed, keep it out of the main table or mark `公开资料未确认`.

### 股权与治理

Use at least two tables when data is available:

| Shareholder | Type | Direct holding | Source |
|---|---|---:|---|

| Person | Role | Direct holding | Indirect path | Calculated total | Confidence |
|---|---|---:|---|---:|---|

For indirect holdings, show formulas such as `平台持股 25.1748% × 个人持有平台 40.3% = 10.1455%`.

### 产品、技术与业务

List confirmed product lines, technical focus, patents/trademarks, customers/partners, and commercialization claims. Separate company-stated positioning from independently corroborated facts.

## 3. 人物背景

### 个人身份与教育/学术

Confirm the person with identity anchors. Include school, degree, lab/department, research direction, publications/patents, and awards only when sourced.

### 与目标公司的关系

Explain the relationship using dated evidence: founder, chairman, director, shareholder, advisor, investor, inventor, or partner. Avoid implying control from title alone unless equity/governance supports it.

### 外部任职时间表

Use a table:

| Company/Institution | Role | Start | End/Status | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Write exact dates where public data gives exact dates. Use `YYYY-MM` or `YYYY` only when that is the public precision.

### 关联网络

Summarize repeated co-investors, co-directors, labs, funds, subsidiaries, and investment vehicles. Keep unsupported network guesses out of diagrams.

## 4. Diagrams

Choose diagrams based on evidence:

- Ownership graph: company, platforms, individuals, direct and indirect percentages.
- Timeline: company events plus person appointments when chronology matters.
- Relationship map: person, target company, external roles, funds, universities.
- Product/IP map: product lines, patents, applications, partners.

Every diagram edge must correspond to a table row or source-backed statement.

## 5. 附录：信息来源

End with a source table:

| No. | Source | Publisher | Date/Access date | Supports | URL |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

Number every source. Use descriptive source names rather than bare domains.
